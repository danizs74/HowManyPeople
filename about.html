About Me

Antonio Llamas

My name is Antonio Llamas, I was born in Barcelona 22 years ago. Currently doing “Erasmus” at Bérgamo, Italy. I study Business Administration in Universidad de Barcelona. When I end school I went to Atlanta (USA) and made volunteering there for 4 months. I organized a Entrepreneurship conference 3 years ago in Barcelona (was attended by 200 young people), the speaker was Manuel Bermejo, professor of the IE Business School. I made an internship in Pwc for 5 months in the Tax and Accounting Services department in Barcelona. After that, I went to Istambul, Turkey and I made a month internship in Deloitte in the Financial Advisory department. Also, I made an internship in Bumm Eventos in Córdoba, Spain for 2 months. I try always to be out of my comfort zone.

I love learning from entrepeneurs and leaders. I think the young people has the power to change the world, and to make things happen. In this blog, I will post some stories of people that inspired me, and I think we can learn from them.
